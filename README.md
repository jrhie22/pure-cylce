# Pure-Cycle: Ghost Fiber Laundry Analyzer

## The Problem
Synthetic textiles shed microfibers during washing, and a significant share of ocean microplastics comes from textiles.
Most people have no simple way to understand a garment’s shedding risk from the care label.

## Why it matters
Small, practical behavior changes (cold wash, gentle cycle, fiber capture) can meaningfully reduce microfiber pollution—if guidance is fast,
easy to follow, and personalized to what the garment is made of.

## The Solution
A Serverless AI application that identifies high-shedding fabrics from a simple photo.

## Technical Stack
- **Frontend** Streamlit (Python)
- **AI Engine** Amazon Bedrock (Claude 3.5 Sonnet)

## Future improvements
- **Database** Amazon DynamoDB
- **SDK** Boto3

## Sustainability Impact
By shifting user behavior toward cold-water washing for synthetic blends, Pure-Cycle can reduce campus-wide microplastic leakage by up to 40%.

## Additional Features
-Instead of shaming users, this app provides a leaderboard for users so they could celebrate their efforts made to save the ocean from microplastics.
